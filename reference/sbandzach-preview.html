CHAPTER I

How we got here.

They met in Gainesville. Both Florida Gators. She was finishing her degree. So was he. The Swamp was where everything quietly began.

Sara is from Valdosta, Georgia. Zach is from Tennessee.

Sara just graduated. She's a pharmacist now. Zach is moving with her to Miami — somewhere south of where they began, somewhere new for both of them.

CHAPTER IV

A place by the water.

Reynolds Lake Oconee sits on nineteen thousand acres of Georgia lake country, an hour and a half from Atlanta. The Great Waters course was carved into the shoreline by Jack Nicklaus thirty years ago.

For one weekend in June, it will host a wedding. Friday for drinks. Saturday for the ceremony, the reception, and the long night that follows. Everyone gathers. Everyone stays. The lake holds it all.
